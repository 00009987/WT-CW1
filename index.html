Places of Interest

Magome (Nakasendo)

No need to imagine 17th-century Japan — come to Magome-juku and see it. A bus from Nakatsugawa Station drops you at the beginning of the cobbled road that winds uphill through the town. From seasonal flowers through street-side waterways to enormous working waterwheels, photo opportunities abound.

Kingdom of Lights

Sitting just across from Omura Bay, Huis Ten Bosch in Nagasaki is one of Japan's largest theme parks and is arguably the most beautiful. The Huis Ten Bosch's Kingdom of Lights presents an otherworldly scene of many colors. Millions of light bulbs turn the night into a wonderland, the likes of which you've never seen before.

Motonosumi-jinja Shrine

A spiritual center that's a lot of fun to visit, Motonosumi-jinja Shrine is a must-see attraction. Facing the Sea of Japan, the wind on the cliff tops is often strong and bracing. Take time to observe the Ryugu no Shiofuki, a natural spout of white sea spray produced by the particular rock formation of the cliffs as the waves dash against them.
